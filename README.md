# origin-theory

**起源理论（Origin Theory）**

起源理论提出了一种结构假说，试图解释文明形成和保持稳定所需要的最低条件。

Origin Theory proposes a structural hypothesis to explain the minimal conditions required for civilization to form and remain stable.

---

## Introduction

起源理论并不试图直接描述某一具体文明的全部历史形态，而是试图回答一个更基础的问题：

**一个文明若要形成并保持稳定，最低需要什么结构条件。**

本理论提出，“起源（Origin）”是文明得以成立、维持与延续的最低结构核心。

Rather than describing the full historical form of any specific civilization, Origin Theory addresses a more fundamental question:

**What are the minimal structural conditions required for civilization to emerge and remain stable?**

The theory proposes that **Origin** is the minimal structural core required for civilization to form, sustain order, and continue through change.

---

## Core Structure

**Origin = Boundary + Judgement + Correction**

即：

- **边界（Boundary）**：决定谁属于群体，谁属于外部  
- **判断（Judgement）**：决定群体内部的行为标准  
- **修正（Correction）**：在偏差与冲突出现时，使秩序重新回到稳定状态  

This means:

- **Boundary** determines what belongs to the group and what lies outside it.  
- **Judgement** determines the shared standards of behavior within the group.  
- **Correction** restores order when deviation, error, or conflict appears.

---

## Theoretical Aim

起源理论尝试从结构层面理解以下问题：

- 文明如何形成  
- 文明为何能够稳定  
- 文明为何会分化、竞争与演化  
- 文明何以逼近自身极限  

Origin Theory attempts to provide a structural account of:

- how civilization forms,  
- why it can remain stable,  
- why it diverges, competes, and evolves,  
- and how it approaches its own limits.

---

## Theory Structure

更广义的原点学体系，大致可概括为：

- **《1+1》** —— 最小成立  
- **《人为人》** —— 人之可校  
- **时空假说（Spacetime Hypothesis）** —— 存在稳定  
- **《文明成立假说：原点学》** —— 起源外扩与文明成立  

四者最终收束为更高层级的统一式：

**Individual = Civilization = Existence = Correctability = Origin**

The broader Origin Theory framework may be summarized as follows:

- **《1+1》** — Minimal formation  
- **《人为人》** — Human correctability  
- **Spacetime Hypothesis** — Stability of existence  
- **Civilization Emergence Hypothesis** — Expansion of origin  

These components ultimately converge into the higher-order structural formula:

**Individual = Civilization = Existence = Correctability = Origin**
